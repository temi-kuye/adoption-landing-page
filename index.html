Dogs are kind, loving, and compassionate they forgive everything and never hold a grudge. No matter what a dog is doing, they are always present in the moment. If you watch your dog throughout the day you'll find that your dog is a better person than most humans.

Here are some benefits of having a dog

  • They can help you be calmer, more mindful, and more present in your life
  • They can make you more social and less isolated
  • Just petting a dog can reduce the petter's blood pressure and heart rate (while having a positive effect on the dog as well).
  • Dogs help you stop stressing out.
  • Dogs encourage you to move.
  • Finally, they are just so adorable that you can't help but be happy around them.
